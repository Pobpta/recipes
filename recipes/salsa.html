Salsa

Description

A dip full of flavors and spices! Such a classic and a hit at parties as well!

Ingredients

  • 6 roma(plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, minced
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 pinch ground cumin, or to taste
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients
  2. Step 2: Place tomatoes, onion, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, lime juice, garlic, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Step 3: Stir well until combined.
  4. Step 4: Enjoy!
