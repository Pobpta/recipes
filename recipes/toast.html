4-Ingredient Egg Avocado Toast

Description

This simple recipe will have you feeling full and refreshed when in a time crunch

Ingredients

  • 1 slice sourdough bread, toasted
  • 1/2 cup mashed Haas avocado
  • 1 large hard boiled egg
  • 1 teaspoon Everything Bagel Seasoning

Steps

  1. Step 1: Smear mashed avocado on toast. Sprinkle with everything bagel seasoning. Top with egg. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Step 2: Enjoy!
